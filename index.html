Overview

  5. 5 We feed all of this information into YCharts for real-time daily analysis. We also have an integration with Hamachi, an AI agent for financial advisors, to communicate your model portfolios.
  6. 6 We believe, and the data shows, asset allocation is a more important driver of client total return than security selection.
  7. 7 That is where we focus our value: modifying asset allocation over time. This is not a buy-and-hold portfolio of active funds; it is an active allocation managed by Modelist with clear detail and transparency into why, when, and how positions move.
  8. 8 SmartLeaf provides daily tax overlay not only on individual equities, but also on funds, and will sell a fund from any provider and replace it when appropriate for tax purposes.
  9. 9 The entire account and household can be tax-managed, not just the equity sleeve.
